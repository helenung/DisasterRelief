Seattle: November 12th, 2015, 10AM disaster strikes!


The "Big One" finally hits.
The biggest earthquake to hit Seattle in hundreds of years.
You are a rescue worker, your job is to grab survivors from the wreckage.

Compete against other rescue workers to see who can save the most people in the next 10 minutes.
This is just a demo of the first level.
